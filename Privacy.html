Privacy Policy of Dobu Martial Arts


Introduction

Welcome to Dobu Martial Arts! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data when you interact with our website, services, and other related activities. By using Dobu Martial Arts' website or services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect various types of information from you, including but not limited to:
a. Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address, phone number, date of birth, and other relevant details.
b. Payment Information: When you make a purchase on our website or services, we collect payment details, such as credit card numbers and billing information. Please note that we do not store your payment information; it is securely processed by our third-party payment processors.
c. Website Usage Information: We may collect non-personally identifiable information about your interactions with our website, including IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and browsing behavior.
d. Martial Arts Progress and Preferences: If you enroll in our martial arts classes or workshops, we may collect information about your progress, preferences, and training history to optimize your learning experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:
a. To Provide Services: We use your personal information to offer our martial arts classes, workshops, and related services.
b. Communication: We may use your contact details to send you important updates, newsletters, marketing materials, and other relevant communications related to our services. You can opt-out of receiving such communications at any time.
c. Improving User Experience: We may analyze website usage data to enhance and personalize your experience on our platform.
d. Legal Compliance: We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties without your explicit consent. However, we may share your information with the following entities:
a. Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers to assist us in providing our services. These providers have access to your information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
b. Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests or government investigations.
c. Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Privacy Choices

a. Updating Your Information: You may update or correct your personal information by contacting us directly.
b. Opt-out: If you wish to opt-out of receiving marketing communications or other non-essential communications from us, you can do so by following the instructions provided in the respective communication.
c. Cookies and Tracking: You can modify your browser settings to decline cookies or be notified when cookies are being sent. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Children's Privacy

Dobu Martial Arts' services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store information from individuals under 13 years old. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take appropriate steps to delete such information from our records.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about our privacy practices.
